# SoulWork Institute — Foundation

Foundational framework, philosophy, and institutional architecture for SoulWork Institute and LightBorn Nexus.

---

## Overview

This repository serves as the central orientation layer for the SoulWork Institute (SWI) ecosystem.

It holds the conceptual, philosophical, and structural foundations that inform all projects, curriculum, tools, and systems developed within SWI and LightBorn Nexus.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this repository is to:

- Establish a coherent institutional foundation
- Articulate guiding principles and methodologies
- Provide structural clarity across projects and initiatives
- Serve as a reference point for future development and collaboration

This work integrates multiple domains, including:

- Integrative and naturopathic frameworks (educational, non-clinical)
- Narrative medicine and reflective inquiry
- Systems thinking and institutional design
- Ethical AI and human-centered technology
- Consciousness-informed leadership and development
